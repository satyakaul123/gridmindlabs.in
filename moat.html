OUR APPROACH
Why the Analysis Is Different
GridMind isn't a chatbot you ask questions to. It's a structured analytical process with deterministic computation, live intelligence, domain expertise, and adversarial quality review.
1

Deterministic Computation

IRR, NPV, DSCR, depreciation, debt service — all computed by validated, deterministic formulas. The AI scopes your assumptions; the mathematical engine computes everything else. Fixed code, zero hallucination.

The numbers are correct not because we checked them — but because the computation cannot be wrong.

2

Live Intelligence

Research runs in real-time when you submit your query. Tariff orders, auction results, policy changes — all reflect current conditions, not training data. A growing domain knowledge base compounds across engagements.

Sources cited inline. Every data point verifiable.

3

Domain Expertise

Purpose-built for Indian energy. CERC/SERC tariff norms, SECI auction mechanics, RPO obligations, DSM penalties, GNA rules, CUF benchmarks, degradation curves — all encoded, not guessed.

When GridMind models a solar project, it knows Rajasthan CUF differs from Gujarat, that ALMM affects module selection, and that ISTS waiver changes the interconnection calculus.

4

Adversarial Quality Review

Every deliverable is independently challenged on methodology, assumptions, and arithmetic before delivery. Assumptions stress-tested against benchmarks. Quantitative outputs cross-checked for internal consistency.

Below-threshold work is automatically revised. The quality critique is included in your deliverable set — full transparency.

REGULATORY DEPTH
Built for India's Regulatory Landscape
Central and state regulators, each with distinct tariff methodologies and compliance frameworks. GridMind covers them all.

CERC

Central tariff determination, interstate transmission, DSM framework, ancillary services

State ERCs (28)

State-level tariff orders, retail supply, intra-state open access, net metering

SECI

RE auction administration, PPA structures, ISTS waivers, hybrid and RTC tenders

MNRE

Technology policy, ALMM, solar/wind/hydrogen incentives, PLI schemes

CEA

Technical standards, grid code, energy storage guidelines, thermal flexibility norms

CONTINUOUS LEARNING
Every Engagement Makes the Next One Better

Every engagement sharpens the next. Lessons — which assumptions held, which data sources proved reliable — are extracted and applied automatically. Client data stays isolated; analytical judgment compounds.
